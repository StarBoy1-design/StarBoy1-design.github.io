Kizumonogatrai Part 3: Reiketsu-Hen

Run time: 1h 23m       Realeased: January 2017      Rating:★★★★★ (4.8 stars) 100% audiance score


Synopsis:Reiketsu concludes the trilogy with Koyomi Araragi restoring the vampire Kiss-shot, only to discover she intends to resume hunting humans, forcing him into a desperate fight to stop her. With Tsubasa Hanekawa's help, Araragi confronts his master in a brutal battle, ultimately choosing a middle path suggested by Meme Oshino to coexist as a near-human while feeding Kiss-shot his blood to keep her alive.
